Our Exclusive Destinations

Discover the hidden treasures of Himachal Pradesh, away from the tourist crowds.

Chanshal Pass

Elevation: 14,830 ft

Experience the highest motorable pass in Shimla district, offering breathtaking views of snow-capped peaks and pristine valleys.

  • Best Time: May to October
  • Activities: Off-roading, Photography, Camping
  • Location: Shimla District
Explore More

Kinnaur Valley

Land of Gods

Discover the mystical valley known for its apple orchards, ancient temples, and stunning mountain landscapes.

  • Best Time: April to October
  • Activities: Temple Visits, Apple Picking, Photography
  • Location: Kinnaur District
Explore More

Dodra Kwar Village

The Last Village

Visit one of the most remote and pristine villages in Himachal, known for its unique culture and traditional architecture.

  • Best Time: June to September
  • Activities: Village Tours, Cultural Experience
  • Location: Shimla District
Explore More

Chandartaal-Nahan Trek

A mesmerizing journey through diverse landscapes and pristine lakes.

Trek Details

  • Difficulty: Moderate to Difficult
  • Duration: 5-6 days
  • Maximum Altitude: 14,100 ft
  • Best Season: June to September

Experience the mesmerizing journey through diverse landscapes, pristine lakes, and stunning mountain views. The trek offers a perfect blend of adventure and natural beauty.
