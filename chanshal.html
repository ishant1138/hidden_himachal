About Chanshal Pass

Chanshal Pass, also known as Chanshal Valley, links Dodra Kwar and Rohru (Chirgaon) in the Shimla district of Himachal Pradesh. The pass sits atop Chanshal Peak, which is the highest peak in the Shimla district. Open from May to November, this majestic pass remains snow-covered for the rest of the year.

Chanshal Peak

The highest peak in Shimla district offering panoramic views

  • Elevation: 4,520m (14,830 ft)
  • Location: Shimla District
  • Best Time: May-November

Dodra Kwar Route

The scenic route connecting Rohru to Dodra Kwar

  • Distance: 180 km from Shimla
  • Limited Bus Service Available
  • Accessible: May-November

Chanshal Valley

A paradise for snow lovers and adventure enthusiasts

  • Activities: Trekking, Photography
  • Snow Season: Dec-April
  • Peak Height: 17,000 ft

Routes to Chanshal Pass

Two main routes connect Shimla to Chanshal Valley:

  • Route 1: Shimla-Theog-Kotkhai-Kharapathar-Hatkoti-Rohru-Larot-Valley (160 km)
  • Route 2: Shimla-Theog-Narkanda-Sungri-Rohru-Larot-Valley (175 km)
  • Regular bus service from Rohru
  • Well-maintained seasonal road
  • Stunning Himalayan views
