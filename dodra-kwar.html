About Dodra Kwar

Nestled in the remote corners of Shimla district, Dodra Kwar is a hidden paradise that remains largely untouched by modern tourism. This pristine valley, divided into two villages - Dodra and Kwar, offers an authentic Himalayan experience with its untouched natural beauty and rich cultural heritage.

Dodra Village

The first village of the valley, known for its traditional wooden houses, apple orchards, and warm hospitality of the local people.

  • Upper Valley
  • Altitude: 2,300m
  • Best Time: April-October

Kwar Village

The second village, offering breathtaking views of the Pabbar River and surrounding mountains, perfect for nature photography and peaceful retreats.

  • Lower Valley
  • Altitude: 2,100m
  • Best Time: May-September

Pabbar River

A pristine river flowing through the valley, offering opportunities for fishing, riverside camping, and stunning photography spots.

  • Valley Center
  • River Activities
  • Photography Hotspot
